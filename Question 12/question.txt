12. Factorial divisions:
	Desc:
		Let s(n) be the smallest number m such that n divides m!.
		So s(10)=5 and s(25)=10.
		Let S(n) be Summation(s(i)) for 2 ≤ i ≤ n.
		For example: S(100)=2012.
		For any given N, print S(N)
	input:
		N [ integer ]

	constraints:
		N<2000000

	output:
		S [ integer ]

	sample io:
		. 5
		. 2012
